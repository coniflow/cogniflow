Features

Everything You Need to Think Better

Capture, connect, and reflect on your ideas with powerful tools that work together seamlessly.

Quick Capture

Press Ctrl+Shift+C anywhere to instantly capture thoughts, voice, or screenshots.

AI Processing

Local AI summarizes, tags, and extracts entities from your notes. Private and fast.

Knowledge Graph

Interactive graph showing connections between notes, tags, and concepts.

Focus Mode

Pomodoro timer with ambient sounds and optional website blocker.

Semantic Search

AI-powered semantic search across all your notes. Find what you need instantly.

Voice Recording

Record voice notes that get transcribed and processed automatically.

Screenshot OCR

Capture screenshots and extract text. Save visual info as searchable notes.

100% Private
